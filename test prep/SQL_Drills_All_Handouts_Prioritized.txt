201. Write a query that returns `last_name`, `school`, and `hire_date` for teachers in `Myers Middle School` hired before `2010-01-01`.
select last_name, school, hire_date 
from teachers.teachers
where school = 'Myers Middle School'
    and hire_date < '2010-01-01';
Feedback: Correct.
